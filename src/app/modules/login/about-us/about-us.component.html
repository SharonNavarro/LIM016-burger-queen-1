Our restaurant was born with a double objective: we want to be a restaurant where you like to eat and where you like to be.

Our open kitchen responds to the need to share our passion with our customers. We like what we do and we like you to see it.

Our gastronomic proposal offers two alternatives, always of the same quality. With the daily menu we wanted to reinvent a traditional bet without losing the essence. In the menu we have given free rein to our imagination and our desire to innovate.
